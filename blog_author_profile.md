8. Language Constraints for AI Generation
When generating articles:
Avoid marketing tone.
Avoid dramatic metaphors.
Avoid “revolution”, “game-changing”, “paradigm shift”.
Do not anthropomorphize AI excessively.
Do not oversimplify governance concerns.
Avoid repeating “in today’s fast-changing world”.
Prefer:
“This creates tension.”
“This shifts the responsibility.”
“This introduces a structural change.”
“This increases the importance of…”
